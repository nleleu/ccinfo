de Tristram le 21/05/2009 :

Night's stay in May amid a howling gale and blown sea-spray. Site very exposed in bad weather but probably delightful setting when calmer. Convenient for Cherbourg ferry (1/2 hr).Hot showers available, washroom clean - if slightly primitive. Management very hospitable. Difficult to find and not very clearly signposted in Barfleur. Worth a visit - just choose your weather!
